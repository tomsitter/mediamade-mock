Team

Greg Smith - Visual Director / Senior Editor

Graduating with honours from the well established Confederation College Film Production Program, Chris has since gone on to establish a name for himself within the South Western Ontario media scene. Past credits include: projects for MTV and CTV, work with the major Canadian YouTube channel "WatchMojo”, Adam Sandler’s “PIXELS” (2015), “ROBOCOP” (2014) and numerous Lifetime and Hallmark Television Movies.

For the past year Chris has taken on the role of Internet and Used Car Manager at an Ontario dealership where his love of business, passion for media, and understanding of inbound marketing went hand and hand producing an online dealership presence in Canada which is expanding vastly compared to that of the manufacturer.

Kyle Rozic - Producer/Director

Upon graduating Confederation College's film production program, Kyle went on to receive a spot on the deans list honour roll at Fanshawe's Advanced Film Production Program. He excelled in the Program’s hands-on approach and thrived in the logistic, budgeting, creative, and marketing roles within media production.

Kyle took on the position of production manager at the award winning and innovative media production company Two Story Productions while working closely with the established ad agency, Hogan Millar Media. Kyle was involved with projects from the initial stages of researching and creative development, through to the actual production and post-production processes. It was here that Kyle was able to utilize his wide set of skills and grow as a creative thinker, account handler, and media producer.
